WWII Veteran Interviews (GDTC Veteran's Voices)

    Project Description

    This documentry project of personal oral histories of Georgian veterans of WWII contains video information collected in Georgia in 2012-2013.

    There are eleven video interviews of approximately 45 minutes to 120 minutes. Interviewees talk about their experiences during WW II. Interviewees also comment on events in the larger context in which they found themselves. Ten of the interviews are conducted in Georgian. One of the interviews in conducted in Russian. All of the interviews were conducted in the presence of a Georgian interpreter. Interview questions were asked in English and reinterpreted into Georgian or Russian.

    Project goals

    • Make WWII oral histories available in video format.
    • Transcribe the interviews into Georgian, Russian, and English.
    • Make more widely available the experience of Georgian and Russian individuals during WWII.
